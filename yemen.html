The mechanism is run by OCHA’s Deconfliction Liaison Team (DLT). This unit serves as a conduit between UN humanitarian organisations and NGOs that wish to share their data (GPS coordinates and/or movement information), and the Evacuation and Humanitarian Operations Committee (EHOC) of the Saudi-Led Coalition.

For a land movement, a UN agency or NGO that wants to notify the Saudi-Led Coalition will use the dedicated platform and submit a form 48 hours in advance, or 24 in an emergency case, with accurate information on the details of the movement, ranging from the itinerary to the vehicles.

The DLT receives the information and submits it to the Coalition, promptly relaying back to the agency whether the Coalition has acknowledged the movement or not.
As of September 2019, the DLT has submitted over 26,ooo notifications on behalf of humanitarian partners in Yemen with an acknowledgement rate, on the part of the Coalition, of 94.1%.
