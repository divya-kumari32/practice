Afghanistan remains among the top five most dangerous countries to be an aid worker. As of September 2020, the Humanitarian Access Group has reported 18 aid workers killed, 43 injured and 95 abducted over the course of the year. There has been a 62 per cent increase in humanitarian access incidents compared to 2019, with national NGOs being the most impacted.
